K-pop舞蹈是韩国流行文化的一部分，是一种充满能量、富有个性和创意的舞蹈形式。通常与流行音乐和韩国偶像文化相结合，形成了独特的音乐和舞蹈风格。

K-pop dance is a part of Korean pop culture and is an energetic, individualistic, and creative form of dance. K-pop dance is often combined with popular music and Korean idol culture, creating a unique style of music and dance.
